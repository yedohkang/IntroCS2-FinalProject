Description

Our website will help indecisive high school students determine their top potential major and corresponding college by taking a quiz and a questionnaire. They will also be able to create an account so that they can login to retake their major quiz or re-enter their new information and get their updated results if their interests or information change. However, we cannot guarantee that they will get in to that college or pursue that specific major in real life, as there was only a select number of colleges and majors.
